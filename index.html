TLV WORKSPACE RANKINGS

CRITERIA

There are many traits of a workspace one could use for review purposes and these will be highly dependant on taste and work purpose.

For each review I assume the user will be working on a laptop over a 5 hour duration starting between 10am-2pm, requiring coffee and 1 meal

Therefore, metrics in use:

  1. Space
  2. Food Availability
  3. Socket Availability

Friend of website Jamie has proposed two metrics, now noted but not included in final ranking (I leave their significance to reader discretion):

  • light
  • temperature control (AC cooling in summer, heat in winter)

refer to website addendum for these

Intentionally omitted:

  • cost (pretty standard across the board)
  • noise (controllable by use of headphones)

Rating based on Wifi strength is contested. I argue one can hotspot in a pinch

addendum

How ratings are decided

Space:

  • 1 - 3 a single ideal space is unlikely to be found
  • 4 - 7 a single space is likely to be found but perhaps not at table or near a socket
  • 8 - 10 multiple spaces are likely to be found at a table near a socket

Food Availability

  • simply on variety of hot and cold dishes. Vegan options are standard.

Socket Availability:

  • 1 - 3 Sockets fought over
  • 4 - 7 a single socket/likely guaranteed
  • 8 - 10 multiple sockets likely (special mention to Dede's 1.25 sockets per seat outdoors)
